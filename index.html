Understanding Your Monthly Mortgage Payment (PITI)

A real estate monthly payment consists of more than just paying back the bank loan. In the U.S. housing market, buyers use the acronym PITI, which stands for Principal, Interest, Taxes, and Insurance. Our free advanced calculator factors in all these hidden variables so you can know exactly what you will owe each month.

What is PMI and When Does it Apply?

Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI) is a safeguard required by conventional lenders when your down payment is less than 20% of the home's purchase price. Once you build 20% equity in your property, this fee is removed. Our tool dynamically tracks your down payment ratio to accurately estimate your monthly PMI dues.
