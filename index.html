How to Use Our Mortgage Calculator

Buying a home is one of the most significant financial decisions you will ever make. Our free Advanced Mortgage Calculator helps you estimate your monthly mortgage payments with ease. Simply enter the home price, your down payment, the current interest rate, and the loan term. The tool will instantly provide a breakdown of your principal and interest payments.

Why is the Down Payment Important?

Your down payment directly impacts your monthly mortgage cost and the total interest you will pay over the life of the loan. A larger down payment reduces the principal amount borrowed, which means lower monthly payments and less total interest paid.
